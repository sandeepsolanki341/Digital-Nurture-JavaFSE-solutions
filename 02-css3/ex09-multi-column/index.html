Community Bulletin

The city council announced a series of free community workshops running through the summer. Residents can register online via the portal. Topics include digital literacy, urban gardening, and small-business finance. Sessions are held at the central library and the east-side community centre. Seats are limited and allocated on a first-come basis. Feedback from last year's programme was overwhelmingly positive, with most attendees rating the sessions four stars or higher. Organisers plan to expand the schedule based on demand and to add weekend slots for working residents.
